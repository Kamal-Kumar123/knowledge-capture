**Last updated:** July 15, 2026  
**Extension name:** Knowledge Capture

## Overview

Knowledge Capture ("we", "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that lets you save text you select on webpages into local documents. This privacy policy explains what data the extension collects, how it is used, and your choices.

By installing and using Knowledge Capture, you agree to this privacy policy.

---

## Summary

- **Local use does not require an account.** Your documents are stored in your browser.
- **We do not sell your data.**
- **We do not track your browsing history.**
- **Google sign-in is optional** and only used if you enable Google Docs sync.

---

## Data We Collect

### 1. Website content (when you save a capture)

When you select text on a webpage and click Save, the extension stores:

- The text you selected
- The page title of the current tab
- The page URL of the current tab

This data is saved **only when you take action** to save a capture. The extension does not automatically collect content from pages you visit.

**Where it is stored:** Locally in your browser using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API.

---

### 2. App settings and documents

The extension stores locally:

- Your documents and their content
- Your active document selection
- Whether capture is enabled or disabled
- Links between local documents and Google Docs (if you use sync)

**Where it is stored:** Locally in your browser.

---

### 3. Personally identifiable information (optional — Google sign-in)

If you choose to connect your Google account for Google Docs sync, the extension may access:

- Your Google account email address (to show which account is connected)

**Where it is stored:** Locally in your browser as part of your auth session. It is not sent to our servers.

---

### 4. Authentication information (optional — Google sign-in)

If you connect Google, the extension stores an OAuth access token locally so you stay signed in and can sync to Google Docs.

**Where it is stored:** Locally in your browser. Tokens are not sent to our servers.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

Knowledge Capture does **not** collect:

- Health information
- Financial or payment information
- Personal communications (emails, chats, messages)
- Location data (GPS, IP address)
- Full web browsing history
- Keystrokes, mouse movements, clicks, or scroll activity beyond your explicit text selection
- Passwords or credentials (other than OAuth tokens managed by Google sign-in)

---

## How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used only to provide the extension's core features:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Selected text | Save to your local document |
| Page title & URL | Provide context for your capture |
| Documents & settings | Manage your notebooks and preferences |
| Google email & token | Optional Google Docs sync (only if you connect) |

We do **not** use your data for:

- Advertising
- Selling to third parties
- Credit scoring or lending decisions
- Any purpose unrelated to note-taking and document capture

---

## Google Docs Sync (Optional)

If you choose to connect your Google account:

- Captures may be synced to a Google Doc you link in the extension
- Data is sent to **Google's servers** via the Google Docs API
- This is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- You can disconnect Google at any time from the extension settings

We do not operate any backend servers. Google Docs sync communicates directly between your browser and Google's APIs.

---

## Third Parties

We do **not** sell or transfer your data to third parties.

The only third-party service involved is **Google** (Google Docs / Google Drive APIs), and only when you explicitly connect your Google account.

---

## Data Retention

- **Local data** remains in your browser until you delete documents, clear extension data, or uninstall the extension.
- **Google Docs data** remains in your Google account according to Google's policies.
- **Auth tokens** are removed when you disconnect Google or uninstall the extension.

---

## Your Choices

You can:

- Disable capture at any time in the extension settings
- Delete documents within the extension
- Export documents as DOCX files
- Disconnect your Google account
- Uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data

---

## Children's Privacy

Knowledge Capture is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Security

Data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. When you use Google Docs sync, data is transmitted over HTTPS to Google's servers.
